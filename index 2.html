Places Must Visit

prithvi cafe

I visited in dec 2018

A quaint place, an outdoor seating just adjacent to the theatre within the same premises. You can have a glimpse of the theatre personalities and the crew dining here too, with nary an air of holier-than-thou. Besides these, the patrons are usually young, chirpy, the uppity kinds with the most fashionable dressing sense. The filtering sunlight, the chirping of the birds, the multiple full-grown trees dotting the area, the casual seating arrangement, the open kitchen, the moderate rates, and the variety of cakes on display highlight this place.

Junter Munter

Junter Munter

I visited in may 2

Besides its name, which provides hours of pronunciation-fun (we settled on Junter Munter), this astronomical park full of whopping, wacky instruments is one of the Pink City's finest sights. Jai Singh's indulgent hobby provided his capital with an alarmingly futuristic, proto-sculpture park which is a photographers dream. Mathematicians would get the most from the place, with a further understanding of how the objects might work, but the apparatus succeed on aesthetic grounds too, worthy art objects in their own right. The tickets will also get you into Amber Fort, and there's a big concession for students foreign and native too.

Reviews on Movies

Avengers Endgame
Excellent film to culminate MCU ride

“Avengers: Endgame” is the culmination of a decade of blockbuster filmmaking, the result of years of work from thousands of people. It is designed to be the most blockbuster of all the blockbusters, a movie with a dozen subplots colliding, and familiar faces from over 20 other movies. It really like nothing that Hollywood has produced before, existing not just to acknowledge or exploit the fans of this series, but to reward their love, patience, and undying adoration. The blunt thing you probably want to know most: Its hard to see serious MCU fans walking away from this disappointed. It checks all the boxes, even ticking off a few ones that fans won't expect to be on the list. It is a satisfying end to a chapter of blockbuster history that will be hard to top for pure spectacle. In terms of sheer entertainment value, it is on the higher end of the MCU, a film that elevates its most iconic heroes to the legendary status they deserve and provides a few legitimate thrills along the way.
